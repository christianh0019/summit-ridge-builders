## 8. Our Process
1. **Discovery Session**: We meet to understand your vision, land, budget, and timeline.
2. **Concept & Feasibility**: Preliminary designs are created alongside a high-level budget analysis.
3. **Design & Selections**: Final plans, material selections, and engineering are completed.
4. **Permitting & Construction**: We handle permits and manage the full construction process with weekly updates.
5. **Final Walkthrough & Handover**: Detailed walkthrough, warranty handoff, and post-build support.
